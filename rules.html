Rules & fair play

Read this before your first match. Breaking these rules can get your squad disqualified and banned from future tournaments.

General rules Fair play policy Scoring system Prize payouts Player conduct

General rules

  1. Eligibility. Players must be 16 years or older, or have parental consent, to register and receive cash prizes.
  2. Registration. Squads must register with a valid in-game name, Free Fire UID, email, and phone number before the registration window closes.
  3. Room ID and password are sent by SMS 10 minutes before the match. Late joiners are not re-invited once the match starts.
  4. Device. Mobile devices only. Emulators, controllers, and third-party sensitivity tools are not allowed unless the tournament explicitly says otherwise.
  5. Squad changes. Once registered, you may swap one player per squad up to 2 hours before match start. No swaps after that.

Fair play policy

  • Use of hacks, aimbots, wallhacks, or any modified APK results in an immediate and permanent ban.
  • Teaming with another squad during a match is not allowed and will disqualify both squads.
  • Stream sniping — using another player's live stream to gain positional advantage — is treated as cheating.
  • Emulator use is detected automatically. Verified violations lead to disqualification from the current tournament and a 30-day suspension.
  • Suspected violations are reviewed by the tournament admin team within 24 hours, and both parties are notified of the outcome.

Scoring system

  • Placement points are awarded based on your squad's final rank in each match (1st place earns the most).
  • Kill points — each confirmed elimination adds a fixed number of points to your squad's total.
  • Booyah bonus — winning the match adds a flat bonus on top of placement and kill points.
  • Season leaderboard rank is the sum of points across all matches played that season.
  • Ties are broken by total kills, then by total booyahs.

Prize payouts

  • Winning squads are paid out within 24 hours of the final match, directly to the UPI ID or bank account provided at registration.
  • Prize money is split evenly among squad members unless the squad leader specifies a different split at registration.
  • Any disqualification discovered after payout requires the prize amount to be returned in full.
  • Entry fees for paid tournaments are non-refundable once the registration window closes, except in the case of a cancelled match.

Player conduct

  • Harassment, hate speech, or abusive language toward other players or admins in chat or voice is not tolerated.
  • Impersonating another player's in-game name or UID to register is grounds for disqualification.
  • Admin decisions on disputes are final for that tournament. Repeated disputes without evidence may result in a warning.
  • Questions about a specific match result can be raised with support within 48 hours of the match ending.
